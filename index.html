Easter Brunch at Emily’s House

A five-course Easter brunch menu!

Course 1

Start with a glass of champagne

Guests begin brunch with a chilled glass of champagne or sparkling juice.

Recipe summary: Pour chilled champagne into a flute and garnish with a strawberry slice or orange twist for a simple celebratory drink.

Course 2

Avocado toast or smoked salmon on rye

This savory brunch course gives guests a lighter option before the heavier foods.

Recipe summary: Toast rye bread, spread mashed avocado with lemon and salt, then top with smoked salmon, cracked pepper, and fresh herbs.

Course 3

Chicken fingers

This course adds a warm and filling comfort-food option to the meal.

Recipe summary: Coat chicken strips in seasoned flour, egg, and breadcrumbs, then bake or fry until golden brown and serve with dipping sauce.

Course 4

French fries

A simple side dish that pairs well with the chicken course and adds texture to the meal.

Recipe summary: Slice potatoes, season with oil, salt, pepper, and garlic powder, then bake until crisp and lightly browned.

Course 5

Apple pie

The brunch ends with a traditional dessert that fits the family Easter theme.

Recipe summary: Fill a pie crust with sliced apples, cinnamon, sugar, and butter, cover with top crust, and bake until bubbling and golden.
